FAQ
How do I download after purchase?
After payment you'll be redirected to the receipt page with instant download buttons. Downloads are also available in your dashboard anytime.
What format are the presets in?
All presets are delivered as ZIP files containing Alight Motion compatible preset files.
Do I need to login for free presets?
Yes, a free account is required. Registration is quick and free — no payment needed.
What payment methods are accepted?
QRIS (all Indonesian e-wallets), GoPay, and PayPal for international buyers.
Can I use presets commercially?
Yes. All purchased presets can be used in commercial projects. Free presets are for personal use only.
